The craft correction (2024–2025)

Independent cultivation licenses came online. The market shifted from MSO oligopoly to a split: MSOs dominate volume, independent craft brands are capturing connoisseur loyalty. The "small-batch" claim is now commoditized — differentiation requires proof: awards, genetics access, and earned community trust. Niche now has the strongest proof point in the state.

The attribution problem: Cannabis has no DTC transaction. There is no pixel on a dispensary sale. Standard digital attribution does not exist. All consumer-facing marketing produces leading indicators, not closed-loop revenue attribution.
The geo-spike methodology — best available proxy

Run geo-targeted paid media around specific dispensary zip codes during a strain drop. Measure sell-through velocity at those accounts vs. non-targeted control accounts. A measurable lift validates that media is driving consumer traffic. This must be validated in Phase 2 before scaling spend.
